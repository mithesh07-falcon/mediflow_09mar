# **App Name**: MediFlow

## Core Features:

- Secure Multi-Role Authentication: Patients, doctors, and pharmacists can securely log in/sign up using email or phone number to access their specific functionalities and ensure data privacy.
- Multi-Family Patient Profile Management: A single user (e.g., a family head) can create and manage detailed profiles for multiple family members, including their individual medical histories.
- AI Doctor Recommender Tool: Based on user-inputted symptoms, an AI tool recommends appropriate specialist doctors to streamline the process of finding medical help.
- Appointment Booking & Management: Patients can view doctor availability and book appointments. Doctors can view and manage their patient appointments, accessing relevant medical history for preparation.
- Digital Prescription Workflow: Doctors can digitally generate and share prescriptions. These are securely stored in patient profiles, and pharmacists can access verified prescriptions for fulfillment.
- Smart Medication Scheduling & Alerts: Automatically schedules medication times from digital prescriptions. Patients receive in-app alerts and reminders, with repeated notifications if doses are not marked as taken.
- Medication Compliance Tracking (Doctor View): Doctors can view a patient's medication compliance, including missed doses, helping them to assess treatment effectiveness and make necessary adjustments.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme to convey clarity and professionalism.
- Primary color: A deep, serene blue-purple (#3528BD) that evokes trust and expertise without being overly clinical.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-purple (#F1F0F4) to ensure readability and maintain a clean interface.
- Accent color: A vibrant blue-cyan (#478BEB) to draw attention to interactive elements and important notifications, providing clear visual hierarchy.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif), chosen for its elegant and intellectual character, suitable for section titles and branding.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), selected for its modern, objective, and highly readable qualities, ideal for displaying detailed medical information and user interfaces.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons that support the functionality without relying on abstract or overly decorative designs, emphasizing usability in a healthcare context.
- Adopt a clean, structured layout with ample whitespace to minimize cognitive load, particularly important for displaying complex medical data and catering to diverse age groups.
